AboutContender Promotions Fight Federation

The most exciting kickboxing event in the UK, CPFF, returns November 27th. Our home-grown talent lights up this electric K-1 spectacle with multiple Contender combatants entering the ring. This thrilling show features pro superstar Nathan Dryden, as well as semi-pro phenom, Matt Richardson both fighting on home turf. And, our stacked undercard of amateur fighters such as Aaron Khan, Harvey Clark and Kai Bell (Contender's 'Young gun' amateur) will be battling to continue their already-impressive records.

Finally, for the first time in CPFF history we're showcasing high-level grappling with a submission-victory-only bout between Andy Jenkins and David Sa.

CPFF 37 promises to be a card full of fire from the very first bell.

  • Why choose CPFF 37?

    In an effort to deliver the most explosive technical combat CPFF 37 will feature:

    • Professional K-1

      Contender pro-fights are fought over 3 gruelling 3 minute rounds with spinning backfists and knees to the head allowed.

    • Submission Grappling

      A No-Gi bout featuring a single 10 minute round with standing/ground submissions, throws and takedowns allowed. A winner will be declared by submission only.

    • Exciting Undercard

      As usual CPFF will showcase electric bouts from impressive new talent. Including Teen, Semi-pro and amateur super-fights.
